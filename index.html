About Trust-IT

A senior engineering team that ships like a startup, scales like an enterprise.

Founded in 2017, Trust-IT was built on a simple idea: software should be reliable by default. We work as an extension of your team — embedded, accountable, and obsessed with the details that separate prototypes from production systems.

Today, we partner with founders, CTOs, and Fortune 500 product teams across fintech, health, and SaaS to build systems that don't break at 3am.

  • Senior engineers only — no juniors learning on your dime
  • Fixed-scope or embedded — your call
  • Transparent weekly demos & reporting
  • You own the code, IP, and infrastructure — always
